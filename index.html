About Me

Hello, I am Hailey Zummo, a junior UX/UI designer looking for an opportunity to showcase my skills. I come from an anthropology background that gives me unique skills when it comes to user-friendly design. Studying the development of humans across a multitude of backgrounds has allowed me to observe real-world problems and start formulating different angles to approach them. I am about focusing on the journey, not simply the destination. I am a hard worker that won’t quit especially when it comes to solving user problems. My ideal client is open-minded… I specialize in human interaction… I enjoy making everyday user frustrations easier… My skills include great communication and interpersonal skills.
